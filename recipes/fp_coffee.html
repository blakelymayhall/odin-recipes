French Press Coffee

Description:

My wife got me a french press and its pretty cool.

Ingredients:

  • Coffee Beans
  • Water

Recipe:

  1. Grind the beans with coarse setting.
  2. While the beans are grinding (or do this in reverse order) heat some water just under boiling.
  3. Put the grinds in the bottom of the french press.
  4. Pour the water in the french press and give it a stir.
  5. Wait four minutes, then press down on the plunger.
  6. Pour and enjoy
