Deviled Eggs

Description:

This is the only way I would eat 6 eggs in one sitting.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Paprika
  • Vinegar

Recipe:

  1. Hard boild the eggs.
  2. Cut in half and scoop out the yolks into a bowl.
  3. Mix the yolks with mayo, mustard, paprika, and vinegar.
  4. Replace in the half-eggs and serve.
